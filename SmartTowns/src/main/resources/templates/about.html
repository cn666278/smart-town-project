Cardiff Town

Our city is proud of its strong community spirit and rich history and culture. Walk along Cardiff Bay, starting at the Bay, visiting many national retailers and independent shops along the way. There is a great variety of food and drink to enjoy in the city centre. Experience the historic charm of Cardiff Castle, followed by the beautiful Cardiff University, where you can experience the passion of students from all over the world.
